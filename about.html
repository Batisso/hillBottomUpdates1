Our Story

The idea was first conceptualizes in 1991 during our service with the UNHCR in Uganda, Hill Bottom Recreation Center became a reality in 2007. Inspired by the Ethiopian song "Gara Sir New Betish," our center offers a wide range of sports and health facilities to the emerging middle class in Addis Ababa.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to meet the growing needs of the community. With the influx of younger residents, we've transformed Hill Bottom into a village of its own, featuring luxury middle-class apartments alongside top-notch health and sports facilities. Welcome to a world of well-being and modern living at Hill Bottom.
